📖 About 8BFR

🎵 8BFR Music Network

A music-first social network built by creators, for creators. Where music meets community meets opportunity.

🎯 Our Mission

8BFR exists to give independent artists, beatmakers, producers, and music lovers a platform that actually works for them. No gatekeepers. No labels deciding who gets heard. Just music, community, and real connections.

We use an algorithm — but unlike other platforms, ours is designed to help independent creators get discovered, not bury them. Every song uploaded gets played on our community radio. The more your audience engages, the higher you climb in our charts.

💡 What Makes 8BFR Different

80/20 Honor System: When you sell music or beats on 8BFR, you receive 100% of the payment directly from the buyer via PayPal. As an honor system, sellers send 20% to 8BFR to support the platform. This isn't enforced through fees or cuts — it's built on trust between creators and the platform that supports them.

Real Community: Feed, chat rooms, DMs, stories, groups, pages, playlists, dedications — this isn't just a music player, it's a full social network built around music.

Beat Store: Producers upload their own beats, set their own prices, and provide their own license agreements. Buyers pay producers directly. 8BFR is a gateway, not a middleman.

Verified Artists & Beatmakers: Submit your Spotify link to get verified. Verified creators can upload and sell music on the platform. Verification is reviewed by the site owner.

Creator Tools: AI-powered tools for album covers, music promotion, translation, writing, and more — available to all users through Producer AI and Owner Studio.

Radio: Continuous play of community-uploaded music. Every song gets heard. No payola, no pay-to-play.

Charts & Algorithm: Songs are ranked by plays, likes, and engagement. The algorithm rewards real listener activity — not ad spend or label deals.

Games & Coins: Earn coins through activity and games, or buy them in the Coin Shop. Spend them on tips, stickers, upgrades, and more.

💰 How Money Works on 8BFR

Selling Music & Beats: Buyers pay sellers directly via PayPal. Sellers receive 100% at the point of sale, then honor the 80/20 split by sending 20% to 8bfr.music@gmail.com. If a seller consistently doesn't honor the split, the owner reserves the right to revoke selling privileges.

Subscriptions: Optional paid tiers unlock premium features like the verified badge, profile upgrades, and enhanced visibility. The platform itself is free to use.

Coins: Earn coins through activity, games, and referrals — or purchase them in the Coin Shop. Coins can be used for tipping other users, buying stickers, and in-platform perks. You never need to buy coins to use the platform, but the option is there if you want more.

Disclaimer: 8BFR, 8BFR Music Network, and James J. Siburt are not responsible for refunds, license disputes, or copyright issues between buyers and sellers. All transactions are between the parties involved.

👤 The Founder

8BFR was created by James Siburt — a music lover who got tired of platforms that don't care about independent artists. Built from scratch on a phone using Termux, Supabase, and pure determination.

This platform is a labor of love. Every feature, every page, every line of code was designed with creators in mind.

🛡️ Age-Based Access

8BFR takes safety seriously. We use a three-tier age system to protect younger users while giving everyone the best experience:

🧸 KidZone (12 & Under) — A fully separate, moderated creative space. Parent-verified accounts only. No main site access, no DMs, no purchases.

🔵 Teen (13-15) — Main site access with parental consent. Restricted features: no tipping, no purchases, no live. DMs limited to teens and parent. 2-hour daily default.

🟣 Full Access (16+) — Everything unlocked.

This policy complies with COPPA and emerging state laws requiring parental consent for users under 16. Read our full age policy →

👁️ Safety & Monitoring

The platform owner monitors all public and private communications for safety purposes, including DMs, community chat, and all KidZone activity. This may be done anonymously. Any illegal activity — especially involving minors — results in an immediate permanent ban and law enforcement referral.

We take child safety extremely seriously. See our Community Rules and Terms of Service for full details.
