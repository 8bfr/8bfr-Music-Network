About 8BFR Music Network

8BFR Music Network is a music-first social platform built for independent artists, producers, beatmakers, and music fans. Founded in 2025, 8BFR provides a space where creators can upload music, sell beats, collaborate, and grow their audience without the noise of mainstream social media.

Unlike traditional platforms, 8BFR puts music at the center of every interaction. With built-in AI tools, a beat marketplace, real-time chat rooms, and a community-driven algorithm, 8BFR is designed by musicians, for musicians.

Features

For Artists: Upload and sell music, build a fanbase, track your algorithm score, collaborate with producers, go live, and use AI tools including the Mastering Studio, File Converter, and 30+ tools in AI Studio.

For Producers: List beats in the Beat Store with tiered licensing, master tracks in-browser, connect with artists, and earn revenue directly via PayPal.

For Fans: Discover new music, follow artists, join groups, chat in real-time, earn coins through games, and invite friends with the referral system.

KidZone: A fully moderated, parent-verified safe space for young music creators to learn, play, and create.
