About 8BFR Music Network

8BFR Music Network is an independent music social platform connecting artists, beatmakers, producers, and fans worldwide. Our mission is to give independent creators the tools and community they need to grow their careers.

Key Facts

Founded: 2025

Platform type: Music-focused social network

Features: Music sharing, beat marketplace, live streaming, community feed, games, AI production tools, coin economy

Revenue model: 80/20 honor-based split on music sales, optional premium upgrades, ad-supported games

Website: 8bfr.com
