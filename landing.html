Meet James J. Siburt (a.k.a. 8bfr)

8BFR Music Network wasn't built by a Silicon Valley team. It was built solo from a phone by a working indie artist who got tired of mainstream platforms taking 70% and giving musicians scraps.

James has released music on Apple Music, Spotify, Tidal, Amazon Music, Audiomack, SoundCloud, and more under two artist names — with features including Method Man & Redman. He built 8BFR because the platform he wanted didn't exist.
