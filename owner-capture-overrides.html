About Capture Protection

By default, all regular users are blocked from right-click, DevTools, drag-save, and get warnings when they try to screenshot or record. This protects creator music, photos, and videos from casual theft.

Staff (owner/co-owner/admin/mod/g7) are always exempt. Grant overrides here for specific users who need temporary access — e.g. for promo, podcasts, or official reviews.
