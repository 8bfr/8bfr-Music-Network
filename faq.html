Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about 8BFR Music Network.

General
What is 8BFR Music Network? ▼
8BFR is a music-first social network for independent artists, beatmakers, producers, and music fans. It combines music streaming, a beat store, social features like feed, chat, DMs, groups, pages, and stories, plus AI creator tools, games, and more in one platform.
Is 8BFR free to use? ▼
Yes. The platform is free to join and use. You can earn coins through activity and games, or purchase them in the Coin Shop. Optional paid subscriptions are available for premium features like the verified badge and profile upgrades, but they are never required.
Who created 8BFR? ▼
8BFR was created by James Siburt, an independent artist and developer who built the entire platform from a phone using Termux. It is a passion project designed to give independent creators a real platform.
Money, Sales and the 80/20 System
How does selling music and beats work? ▼
Verified artists and beatmakers can upload and sell music directly on 8BFR. Buyers pay sellers directly via PayPal. 8BFR does not process payments. The seller receives 100% of the payment at the point of sale.
What is the 80/20 Honor System? ▼
After receiving payment from a buyer, the seller sends 20% to 8bfr.music@gmail.com via PayPal to support the platform. The seller keeps 80%. This is an honor system. It is not automatically deducted. If a seller consistently does not honor the split, the site owner reserves the right to revoke their selling privileges.
How does the Beat Store work? ▼
Beatmakers upload their beats, set their own prices, and upload their own license agreements for MP3 Lease, WAV Lease, Trackout, and Exclusive, each with separate terms. Buyers browse, preview up to 30 seconds, select a format, and pay the producer directly via PayPal. 8BFR is a gateway, not a party to the transaction. 8BFR and James J. Siburt are not responsible for refunds, license disputes, or copyright issues.
What are the paid subscriptions? ▼
Optional paid tiers include: VIP at $4.99 per month for enhanced profile features, Creator Pro at $9.99 per month for advanced creator tools and visibility, and Elite at $19.99 per month for maximum perks. These are optional. You can use 8BFR fully without subscribing. See the Pricing page for details.
What can I do with coins? ▼
Coins can be used to tip other users, buy stickers, purchase in-game items, and unlock profile perks. You earn coins through platform activity like posting, engaging, playing games, and referring friends. You can also buy them in the Coin Shop.
Artists, Beatmakers and Verification
How do I get verified as an artist or beatmaker? ▼
During signup or from your profile, submit a link to your Spotify artist page. The site owner reviews and approves your verification. Once verified, you can upload and sell music on the platform. Verification is free. It just proves you are a real artist.
What is the difference between the verified badge and artist verification? ▼
Artist or Beatmaker verification is free. It proves you are a real creator via Spotify and unlocks upload and sell privileges. The verified badge with checkmark is a separate paid feature available through subscriptions that adds a checkmark to your profile for visibility and credibility. They are independent of each other.
Can I upload music without being verified? ▼
No. Only verified artists and beatmakers can upload music. This protects the platform from copyright issues and ensures quality. You can still enjoy all other features including listening, playlists, community, and games.
What roles can I choose? ▼
Music industry roles like producer, songwriter, DJ, singer, rapper, engineer, dancer, photographer, and more are self-selectable from your profile. However, artist and beatmaker roles are only assigned after the owner verifies you. Staff roles like owner, co-owner, admin, and mod are owner-assigned only.
Algorithm and Charts
Does 8BFR have an algorithm? ▼
Yes. Unlike platforms that bury independent content, our algorithm is designed to help creators get discovered. It factors in plays, likes, shares, and engagement to rank songs in the charts. Every uploaded song also gets played on the community radio. No pay-to-play.
How do the charts work? ▼
Charts rank songs by play count and engagement. Categories include Hot 50, Trending, New Releases, Top Beats, Most Liked, and Most Played. You can filter by time period including this week, this month, and all time. Charts update automatically as listeners engage with music.
Age Requirement and Safety
What is the age requirement? ▼
8BFR is for adults 18 and older. You must be at least 18 to create an account or use any feature of the site. This refers to membership age, not content - 8BFR is not an adult-content platform and explicit material is not permitted. Every member confirms they are 18+ at signup, and we may require photo ID verification to confirm age. See our Age Policy for full details.
Is there monitoring on the platform? ▼
Yes. The platform is monitored for safety purposes. Any illegal activity results in an immediate permanent ban and, where applicable, law enforcement referral.
Features
What is the Radio? ▼
The radio plays community-uploaded songs continuously. Every song uploaded by verified artists gets into the rotation. It uses an algorithm that prioritizes 8BFR platform songs and avoids repeating the same artist back-to-back.
What are Groups and Pages? ▼
Groups are community spaces where members can post, discuss, and connect around shared interests. Group creators are admins and can promote members to moderator. The site owner can take over or transfer ownership of any group.

Pages are public-facing profiles for brands, projects, or creative identities, similar to Facebook Pages. Page owners can post text, photos, and videos.
What AI tools are available? ▼
8BFR offers a full suite of AI-powered tools through AI Studio, available to all users, including lyric writing, beat ideas, album cover generation, music promotion, social media content, translation, and 30+ more tools. Additional standalone tools include the Mastering Studio (real-time EQ, compression, pitch correction, noise removal, A/B preview), File Converter (convert between MP3, WAV, FLAC, OGG with spatial audio enhancement), Stem Splitter (separate vocals, drums, bass), and Music Video Maker. Creator Tools offers background removal, voice-over generation, thumbnail creation, caption writing, hashtag generation, video script writing, and photo retouching for influencers and content creators.
What games are available? ▼
8BFR has a full Multiplayer Arena with 1v1 games including 8-Ball Pool (real physics), Chess, Checkers, Connect 4, Tic-Tac-Toe, Darts, Bowling, and more. Wager coins against opponents, earn rankings from Rookie to Legend, and join tournaments. Game groups let you create private rooms with friends.
How does the ranking system work? ▼
Each game has its own ranking based on wins: Rookie (0-4), Beginner (5-19), Apprentice (20-49), Intermediate (50-99), Advanced (100-199), Skilled (200-499), Veteran (500-999), Expert (1000-2499), Master (2500-4999), Grandmaster (5000-9999), Legend (10000+). Rankings are per-game so you can be a Legend in pool but a Rookie in chess.
Can I make video or voice calls? ▼
Yes! Open any DM conversation and tap the phone or camera icon to start a 1-on-1 voice or video call. Calls are peer-to-peer and free with no time limit.
How do I create a post? ▼
Go to Create Post to record video, take photos, write text posts, or upload media. Features include 13 filters, 11 effects, front/back camera with picture-in-picture, timer, flash, AI photo generation, captions, and duration options from 15 seconds to 10 minutes.
What are Creator Tools? ▼
Creator Tools at creator-tools.html are designed for influencers and content creators. Tools include background removal and replacement, AI voice-over, thumbnail maker, caption writer for TikTok/Instagram/Amazon/YouTube, hashtag generator, video script writer, and photo retouching.
Account
How do I sign up? ▼
Go to Sign Up, choose your roles, enter your details, and verify your email with a 6-digit code. You get 50 free coins to start. If someone referred you with their invite code, you get an extra 100 bonus coins. If you are an artist or beatmaker, you can submit your Spotify link during signup for verification.
Can I change my role or badges? ▼
You can select music industry roles like producer, DJ, songwriter, and community badges like fan, supporter, 8bfrfan from your profile editor. Staff roles and verified badges can only be changed by the site owner.
How do I reset my password? ▼
Go to Reset Password and enter your email. You will receive a link to create a new password.
