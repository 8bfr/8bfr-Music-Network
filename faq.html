❓ FAQ

General

What is 8BFR Music Network? +
8BFR is a music-focused social network where artists, beatmakers, producers, and fans connect. Share music, collaborate, go live, earn coins, and build your audience — all in one place.
Is 8BFR free to use? +
Yes! Creating an account and using core features is completely free. Some premium features like the verified badge, special profile frames, and certain AI tools have optional paid upgrades.
How do I sign up? +
Click Sign Up, choose your roles (artist, beatmaker, fan, etc.), enter your details, and verify your email with the 6-digit code sent to you. Artists and beatmakers need to provide a Spotify link for verification.

Music & Content

How do I upload music? +
Go to your Profile and click the Music tab. You must be a verified artist or beatmaker to upload. Upload your audio file, add cover art, set a price (optional), and publish.
How does the 80/20 revenue split work? +
When someone buys your beat or song, you keep 80% and 8BFR receives 20%. This is an honor-based system — payments go directly to your PayPal. Failure to remit the platform share may result in selling privileges being suspended.
What are coins used for? +
Coins are 8BFR's virtual currency. Use them to tip artists, buy stickers, unlock profile upgrades, and more. Earn coins by playing games, completing challenges, and being active in the community. Coins cannot be exchanged for real money.

Account & Safety

How do I get verified? +
Artist/beatmaker verification is free — provide your Spotify link during signup and the owner will review your request. The verified badge (checkmark) is a separate paid feature.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. We use Supabase for secure authentication and data storage. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We do not sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How do I report someone? +
Use the report button on any message, post, or profile. Reports go directly to our moderation team for review. Serious violations result in immediate action.
What is KidZone? +
KidZone is a protected section for younger users. Parents verify their identity to create child accounts. Kids cannot interact with the main platform or access adult content. All KidZone activity is monitored.
