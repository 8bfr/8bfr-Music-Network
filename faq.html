❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about 8BFR Music Network.

🎵 General
What is 8BFR Music Network? ▼
8BFR is a music-first social network for independent artists, beatmakers, producers, and music fans. It combines music streaming, a beat store, social features (feed, chat, DMs, groups, pages, stories), AI creator tools, games, and more — all in one platform.
Is 8BFR free to use? ▼
Yes. The platform is free to join and use. You can earn coins through activity and games, or purchase them in the Coin Shop. Optional paid subscriptions are available for premium features like the verified badge and profile upgrades, but they're never required.
Who created 8BFR? ▼
8BFR was created by James Siburt, an independent artist and developer who built the entire platform from a phone using Termux. It's a passion project designed to give independent creators a real platform.
💰 Money, Sales & the 80/20 System
How does selling music and beats work? ▼
Verified artists and beatmakers can upload and sell music directly on 8BFR. Buyers pay sellers directly via PayPal — 8BFR does not process payments. The seller receives 100% of the payment at the point of sale.
What is the 80/20 Honor System? ▼
After receiving payment from a buyer, the seller sends 20% to 8bfr.music@gmail.com via PayPal to support the platform. The seller keeps 80%. This is an honor system — it's not automatically deducted. If a seller consistently doesn't honor the split, the site owner reserves the right to revoke their selling privileges.
How does the Beat Store work? ▼
Beatmakers upload their beats, set their own prices, and upload their own license agreements (MP3 Lease, WAV Lease, Trackout, Exclusive — each can have separate terms). Buyers browse, preview (30-second limit), select a format, and pay the producer directly via PayPal. 8BFR is a gateway, not a party to the transaction. 8BFR and James J. Siburt are not responsible for refunds, license disputes, or copyright issues.
What are the paid subscriptions? ▼
Optional paid tiers include: VIP ($4.99/mo) for enhanced profile features, Creator Pro ($9.99/mo) for advanced creator tools and visibility, and Elite ($19.99/mo) for maximum perks. These are optional — you can use 8BFR fully without subscribing. See the Pricing page for details.
What can I do with coins? ▼
Coins can be used to tip other users, buy stickers, purchase in-game items, and unlock profile perks. You earn coins through platform activity (posting, engaging, playing games, referring friends) or you can buy them in the Coin Shop.
🎤 Artists, Beatmakers & Verification
How do I get verified as an artist or beatmaker? ▼
During signup (or from your profile), submit a link to your Spotify artist page. The site owner reviews and approves your verification. Once verified, you can upload and sell music on the platform. Verification is free — it just proves you're a real artist.
What's the difference between the verified badge and artist verification? ▼
Artist/Beatmaker verification is free — it proves you're a real creator via Spotify and unlocks upload/sell privileges. The verified badge (✓) is a separate paid feature available through subscriptions that adds a checkmark to your profile for visibility and credibility. They're independent of each other.
Can I upload music without being verified? ▼
No. Only verified artists and beatmakers can upload music. This protects the platform from copyright issues and ensures quality. You can still enjoy all other features — listening, playlists, community, games, etc.
What roles can I choose? ▼
Music industry roles like producer, songwriter, DJ, singer, rapper, engineer, dancer, photographer, etc. are self-selectable from your profile. However, artist and beatmaker roles are only assigned after the owner verifies you. Staff roles (owner, co-owner, admin, mod) are owner-assigned only.
📊 Algorithm & Charts
Does 8BFR have an algorithm? ▼
Yes. Unlike platforms that bury independent content, our algorithm is designed to help creators get discovered. It factors in plays, likes, shares, and engagement to rank songs in the charts. Every uploaded song also gets played on the community radio — no pay-to-play.
How do the charts work? ▼
Charts rank songs by play count and engagement. Categories include Hot 50, Trending, New Releases, Top Beats, Most Liked, and Most Played. You can filter by time period (this week, this month, all time). Charts update automatically as listeners engage with music.
🛡️ Age System & Safety
How does the age system work? ▼
8BFR uses a three-tier system:

🧸 KidZone (12 & Under) — A fully separate, parent-verified space. No main site access, no DMs, no purchases. Safe, moderated environment for young creators.

🔵 Teen (13-15) — Main site with restrictions. Requires parent/guardian email consent. No tipping, purchases, or live streaming. DMs limited to teens and parent. 2-hour daily default.

🟣 Full Access (16+) — Everything unlocked.

This complies with COPPA and emerging state laws. See our Age Policy for full details.
Is there monitoring on the platform? ▼
Yes. The site owner monitors all communications — including DMs, public chat, group posts, and all KidZone activity — for safety purposes. This may be done anonymously. Any illegal activity, especially involving minors, results in an immediate permanent ban and law enforcement referral.
How does parent verification work for KidZone? ▼
When a child under 13 signs up, their account is placed in KidZone. A parent must verify their identity through our verification system before the account becomes active. The parent badge is only granted after successful ID verification.
🚀 Features
What is the Radio? ▼
The radio plays community-uploaded songs continuously. Every song uploaded by verified artists gets into the rotation. It uses an algorithm that prioritizes 8BFR platform songs and avoids repeating the same artist back-to-back.
What are Groups and Pages? ▼
Groups are community spaces where members can post, discuss, and connect around shared interests. Group creators are admins and can promote members to moderator. The site owner can take over or transfer ownership of any group.

Pages are public-facing profiles for brands, projects, or creative identities — similar to Facebook Pages.
What AI tools are available? ▼
8BFR offers AI-powered tools through Producer AI and Owner Studio including album cover generation, music promotion text, social media content, translation, and writing tools. These are available to all users.
What games are available? ▼
8BFR has a variety of games including pool, arcade, sports, and music games. You can earn coins by playing. Games are available in the main site and a separate safe selection is available in KidZone.
👤 Account
How do I sign up? ▼
Go to Sign Up, choose your roles, enter your details, and verify your email with a 6-digit code. You'll get 50 free coins to start. If you're an artist or beatmaker, you can submit your Spotify link during signup for verification.
Can I change my role or badges? ▼
You can select music industry roles (producer, DJ, songwriter, etc.) and community badges (fan, supporter, 8bfrfan, etc.) from your profile editor. Staff roles and verified badges can only be changed by the site owner.
How do I reset my password? ▼
Go to Reset Password and enter your email. You'll receive a link to create a new password.
