❓ FAQ

🎵 Getting Started
What is 8BFR Music Network?▼

8BFR is a music-first social platform where artists, producers, beatmakers, singers, DJs, engineers, and fans connect. Upload music, sell beats, collaborate, chat, and grow your audience — all in one place.

How do I sign up?▼

Go to the signup page, pick your role (artist, beatmaker, fan, etc.), create your account with email and password. Artists and beatmakers need to provide a Spotify link for verification. All other roles can sign up instantly.

Is 8BFR free?▼

Yes! The core platform is 100% free — uploading, profiles, chat, AI tools, games, and beat store listings. The only paid feature is the optional Verified badge ($4.99/mo) which gives you an algorithm boost and extra perks.

🎹 Music & Beats
How do I upload music?▼

Go to your profile → Music tab → click Upload. Add your MP3 file, cover art (optional), title, artist name, genre, and price. Songs appear immediately in the feed and on the Songs page.

How does the Beat Store work?▼

Producers upload beats and set their own prices across license tiers (MP3 Lease, WAV Lease, Trackout, Exclusive). 8BFR takes 0% — all revenue goes directly to the producer. The 80/20 honor system applies when artists use leased beats commercially.

What file formats are supported?▼

MP3 for audio uploads. Cover art accepts JPG, PNG, and WebP. We recommend 320kbps MP3 for best quality and 1024×1024 for cover art.

👤 Accounts & Profiles
What are badges?▼

Badges show your role and achievements. Role badges (artist, beatmaker, DJ, etc.) are chosen at signup. The Verified badge is a paid monthly subscription. Special badges like Supporter (donated) and Founder are earned through actions.

How does artist/beatmaker verification work?▼

When you select Artist or Beatmaker at signup, you provide your Spotify artist page URL. The owner reviews and approves your verification. Once approved, your artist/beatmaker badge activates and upload tools unlock.

Can I change my role after signing up?▼

Yes. Contact support or the owner through the Help page and your role can be updated. You can hold multiple role badges.

🤖 AI Tools
What AI tools are available?▼

Free tier includes: lyrics writer, caption generator, hashtag generator, bio writer, rhyme finder, translator, grammar fixer, summarizer, meme creator, and more (15 generations/day). Verified users get 50/day. Owner/Co-Owner get unlimited.

Does AI generate actual beats or songs?▼

Currently, the AI generates text (lyrics, ideas, blueprints) and images (album covers). Actual beat and video generation via Suno/Udio/Runway will be available in the future Premium tier.

💬 Chat & Community
How does the chat work?▼

Community Chat has multiple rooms (General, Music Talk, Beat Lab, Collab, Hype). Messages are real-time via Supabase. Staff can moderate — hide messages, mute or kick users. DMs are private one-on-one conversations.

How do I report someone?▼

In DMs, hover over a received message and click the 🚩 flag icon. In the chat room, staff have right-click moderation tools. All reports go directly to the owner for review.

Still have questions?

Reach out on the Help page or DM the owner directly.
