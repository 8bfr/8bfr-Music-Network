🆘 Help Center

Getting Started

Welcome to 8BFR Music Network! Here's how to get the most out of the platform.

Create your account: Sign up with your email, choose your roles, and verify. Artists and beatmakers need a Spotify link.

Set up your profile: Go to Profile → Edit Profile. Add a bio, avatar, cover photo, and connect your social/music platform links.

Start posting: Share music, photos, videos, and text posts from your profile. Pin your best content to the top.

Features Guide

Feed: See posts from the community. Like, comment, reshare, and favorite content.

Live: Post your streaming link when you go live on YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, or other platforms. Members can watch directly on 8BFR.

Radio: Listen to curated music from the 8BFR community.

Games: Play games and earn coins. Up to 50 coins per day from gameplay.

Stories: Share temporary moments that expire after 24 hours.

DMs: Message any member directly from their profile or the DM page.

Music Tools

Upload music: Verified artists/beatmakers can upload songs and beats from the Music tab on their profile.

Beat Store: Browse and purchase beats. Beatmakers set their own prices and licenses.

Playlists: Create and share playlists of your favorite tracks.

Coins & Economy

Earning coins: Play games, complete challenges, daily logins, and positive engagement.

Spending coins: Tip artists, buy stickers, unlock profile upgrades and frames.

Coins are not real currency and cannot be cashed out.
