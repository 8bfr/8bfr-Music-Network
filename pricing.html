💳 Pricing

Free for Everyone

8BFR Music Network is free to join and use. Create your profile, post content, listen to music, play games, earn coins, and connect with the community at no cost.

For Artists & Beatmakers

Verification: Free — submit your Spotify link and get approved by the owner

Music uploads: Free for verified artists and beatmakers

Beat/Song sales: 80/20 honor-based split (you keep 80%)

All production tools: Accessible through Producer AI

Optional Upgrades

Verified Badge (✓): Paid feature — adds a verified checkmark to your profile for credibility

Profile Frames: Animated and special frames available in the Coin Shop

Profile Backgrounds: Custom cover themes available in the Shop

Sticker Packs: Special stickers for posts and comments

Artist Pro Badge: Premium creator badge with enhanced visibility

Coins

Coins are earned through gameplay, engagement, and challenges. They can be spent on profile upgrades, stickers, tips, and more. Coins have no real-money value.

No Hidden Fees

We don't charge subscription fees. We don't sell your data. We don't serve ads in your feed. The platform is supported by music sales commissions, optional upgrades, and game advertising revenue.
