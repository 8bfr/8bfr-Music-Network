❓ Pricing FAQ

Does 8BFR take a cut of sales?

No. 0% platform fee on all beat and song sales. We fund the platform through Verified subscriptions.

What's the 80/20 honor system?

When an artist uses a leased beat and the song blows up, the expectation is 80% artist / 20% producer. It's an honor code, not enforced. Exclusive purchases have no split.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Perks stay active until end of billing period.

Do I need Verified to upload?

No. Free accounts get unlimited uploads, Beat Store listings, and all core features.
