The 80/20 Honor System

How It Works

Buyers pay artists/beatmakers directly via PayPal. You receive 100% of every sale. We trust you to send 8BFR its 20% platform fee afterward.

Song Download: Fan buys for $5 -- $5 goes to YOUR PayPal -- You send $1 (20%) to [email protected] -- You keep $4
Beat Sale: Artist buys beat for $50 -- $50 goes to YOUR PayPal -- You send $10 (20%) -- You keep $40
Royalties: Any royalties from Spotify, Apple Music, etc. are 100% yours. No split on royalties.

The platform tracks all sales and amounts owed. Failure to honor the 20% fee may result in selling privileges being disabled.

Beat Store

Producers set their own prices and upload their own license agreements. No preset license tiers -- you control your licensing terms.

Buyers pay you directly via PayPal. You keep 100% at point of sale, then send 20% to the platform.
