💰 The 80/20 Honor System

How It Works

Buyers pay artists/beatmakers directly via PayPal. You receive 100% of every sale. We trust you to send 8BFR its 20% platform fee afterward. This is called the honor system.

Song Download: Fan buys for $5 → $5 goes to YOUR PayPal → You send $1 (20%) to 8bfr.music@gmail.com → You keep $4 (80%)
Beat Sale: Artist buys beat for $50 → $50 goes to YOUR PayPal → You send $10 (20%) to platform → You keep $40
Stickers / Merch: Same 80/20 split on all direct sales through 8BFR.
Royalties: Any royalties from Spotify, Apple Music, etc. are 100% yours. No split on royalties — only on direct sales made through 8BFR.

The platform tracks all sales and amounts owed. If you consistently fail to honor the 20% fee, selling privileges may be disabled.

🎚️ Beat Store

Producers set their own prices and upload their own license agreements with each beat. There are no preset license tiers — you control your licensing terms.
