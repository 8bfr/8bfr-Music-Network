📋 Community Rules

1. Respect Everyone

Treat all members with respect. No hate speech, racism, sexism, homophobia, or targeted harassment. This is a creative community — lift people up, don't tear them down.

2. No Illegal Content

Do not post illegal content, copyrighted material you don't own, or content that promotes illegal activities. This includes pirated music, stolen beats, and unauthorized recordings.

3. No Spam or Self-Promotion Abuse

Don't flood the feed, chat, or DMs with repetitive promotional content. Share your work naturally and engage with others. Spam bots will be permanently banned.

4. Keep It Clean in Public Spaces

Community chat and the main feed should be kept appropriate. Explicit content must be properly tagged. No pornographic or excessively violent content anywhere on the platform.

5. No Scams or Fraud

Do not attempt to scam other users through fake sales, phishing, or manipulation. The 80/20 honor system for beat/song sales requires honest participation. Scammers will be banned and reported.

6. Protect Children

Any content or behavior that endangers minors will result in immediate permanent ban and report to law enforcement. This is non-negotiable.

7. No Impersonation

Do not create accounts pretending to be someone else — whether another user, a celebrity, or a brand. Parody accounts must be clearly labeled.

8. Report, Don't Retaliate

If someone breaks the rules, use the report button. Don't engage in arguments or retaliation. Let moderators handle it.

9. Honor the Economy

Don't exploit bugs, hack the coin system, or attempt to manipulate the algorithm. Play fair.

Consequences

Violations result in: warning → temporary mute → temporary ban → permanent ban, depending on severity. Serious violations (threats, child safety, illegal activity) result in immediate permanent ban.
