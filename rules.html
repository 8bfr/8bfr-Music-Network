📜 Community Rules

🎵 Golden Rule

8BFR is a music community — keep it about the music, the culture, and lifting each other up.

✅ What's Allowed

• Sharing your music, beats, and creative work

• Constructive feedback and honest opinions

• Collaboration requests and networking

• Self-promotion in appropriate areas (feed, profile, Hype room)

• Respectful debate and discussion

• Explicit content with proper tagging (mark songs as explicit)

🚫 What's NOT Allowed

• Harassment, bullying, threats — zero tolerance, instant ban

• Hate speech — racism, sexism, homophobia, or discrimination of any kind

• Spam — repetitive posts, bot behavior, mass DMs

• Illegal content — pirated music, stolen beats, illegal substances

• NSFW content — no nudity or sexual content in any public area

• Doxxing — sharing someone's personal info without consent

• Scamming — fake sales, phishing, impersonation

• Plagiarism — claiming someone else's work as your own

• Bypassing bans — creating alt accounts to evade a ban

• Lying about your age — providing a false date of birth

⚖️ Consequences

Warning → Mute (temporary) → Kick → Ban (permanent)

Severity matters. Minor offenses get warnings. Serious offenses (threats, hate speech, scamming, anything involving minors) skip straight to ban. All moderation actions are logged.

Staff roles that can moderate: Owner 👑, Co-Owner, Admin 🛡️, Mod 🟢

🎵 Beat Store & Sales Rules

• Only upload beats/songs you own or have rights to

• License terms are between buyer and seller — 8BFR doesn't mediate disputes

• The 80/20 honor system applies to leased beats used commercially

• No fake streams, artificial plays, or engagement manipulation

🔞 Adults Only (18+)

8BFR is for adults 18 and older.

• You must be at least 18 years old to create an account or use any feature

• "Adults only" means membership age — this is NOT an adult-content site

• Explicit sexual content is not permitted (see content rules above)

• Providing a false age is grounds for immediate removal

• Accounts found to belong to minors will be deleted

• View full age policy →

👁️ Monitoring Notice

The platform uses an AI-powered AutoMod system that automatically checks messages for harmful content including harassment, hate speech, threats, and inappropriate material. Flagged content is reviewed by the owner. The platform owner may also manually monitor communications (DMs, chat, posts) for safety purposes. This monitoring may be conducted anonymously. By using 8BFR, you consent to this monitoring as described in our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

⚠️ Any illegal activity involving minors will result in an immediate permanent ban and all available information forwarded to law enforcement.
