🔒 Privacy Policy

Overview

8BFR Music Network ("8BFR", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account data: email, username, display name, date of birth, role selections, Spotify URL (for artist verification), password (encrypted).

Profile data: avatar, bio, social links, badges — all user-provided.

Content: posts, songs, stories, comments, chat messages, direct messages.

Usage data: pages visited, features used, time spent (for teen time limits).

Verification data: For parents — identity verification is processed through Didit, a third-party provider. 8BFR does not store government ID images. For teens — parent email address is collected for consent purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

• To operate and improve the Platform

• To verify user identity and age tier classification

• To enforce age-based access restrictions (KidZone, teen limits)

• To process transactions (beat sales, tips, subscriptions)

• To send important account notifications

• To enforce community rules and safety policies

• To comply with legal requirements regarding minors' data protection

3. Age Tier Data Handling

KidZone (12 & Under): Minimal data collected. No tracking for advertising. No data shared with third parties. Content fully moderated. Parent has full visibility into all collected data via the Parent Hub.

Teen (13-15): Date of birth used for tier classification. Parent email stored for consent flow. Time tracking for daily limits. All data accessible to the teen's verified parent.

Adult (16+): Standard data collection as described above.

We comply with COPPA for users under 13 and emerging state laws for users under 16. See our Age Policy for full details.

4. Platform Monitoring

For safety — especially the safety of minors — the platform owner may review any content on 8BFR including:

• Direct messages between users

• Community chat messages in all rooms

• KidZone posts, uploads, and activity

• Teen account activity

This monitoring may be conducted anonymously. Monitoring is used exclusively for safety enforcement and is not shared with third parties unless required by law (e.g., law enforcement requests related to illegal activity involving minors).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

• Didit — for parent identity verification (processed on their servers)

• Supabase — our database provider (data stored securely)

• Law enforcement — when required by law or when illegal activity involving minors is detected

• Payment processors — PayPal for transactions (we don't store payment details)

6. Content Ownership

You retain ownership of all content you upload (music, images, text, videos). By uploading, you grant 8BFR a non-exclusive, worldwide license to display, distribute, and promote your content on the platform. You can delete your content at any time, which revokes this license.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption for passwords and secure connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission. Authentication is handled by Supabase with row-level security. We do not store government IDs — identity verification is processed by Didit.

8. Your Rights

• Access: You can view all your data through your profile and settings

• Correction: Update your information anytime in Settings

• Deletion: Request account deletion by contacting us

• Parental access: Parents can view and manage all data for their children's accounts

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance. We will notify users of significant changes.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us at 8bfr.network@gmail.com or visit the Help page.
