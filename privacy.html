1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, display name, birthday (month/day/year), and role selection. Profile information you provide (bio, avatar, social links) is stored in our database. We do not sell your data to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve the 8BFR Music Network platform, display your profile to other users, enable social features (friends, follows, messages), process verification requests, and communicate important platform updates.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data as long as your account is active.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - database and authentication
  • EmailJS - email verification during signup
  • Didit - identity verification for KidZone parents
  • Pollinations AI - beat generation and AI tools
  • PayPal - payment processing (direct between buyers and sellers)

Each service has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

8BFR takes children's privacy seriously. Users 12 and under can only access KidZone with verified parental consent. Parents must verify their identity through Didit before creating child accounts. KidZone data is kept separate from the main platform. Children cannot access adult sections, DMs with strangers, or external links. All KidZone activity is logged and monitored. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. Parents can request deletion of their child's data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, delete your account and associated data, export your data, and opt out of non-essential communications. To exercise these rights, contact 8bfr.music@gmail.com

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage and session storage for authentication and user preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising trackers. We do not display third-party ads.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only when required by law or to protect the safety of our users (particularly minors), or with your explicit consent.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to 8bfr.music@gmail.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact 8bfr.music@gmail.com
