🔒 Privacy Policy

Overview

8BFR Music Network ("8BFR", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Effective: March 2026

Data We Collect

Account Information

Email address, username, display name, password (hashed), role selection, and optional Spotify verification URL.

Profile Information

Bio, avatar, banner image, location (optional), badges, and social links you choose to add.

Content

Songs, beats, posts, comments, messages, dedications, and other content you create on the platform.

Usage Data

Play counts, likes, follows, page views, and interaction data used to power the algorithm and charts.

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, device info, and session tokens for authentication.

How We Use Your Data

• Account management — login, authentication, verification

• Platform features — feed algorithm, charts, recommendations, search

• Communication — notifications, password resets, important updates

• Safety — moderation, abuse prevention, report handling

• We do NOT sell your data to third parties

• We do NOT serve targeted advertising based on your data

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Audio files are stored in Supabase Storage with access controls.

Your Rights

• Access — request a copy of your data

• Correction — update inaccurate information via your profile settings

• Deletion — request account deletion by contacting the owner

• Portability — request your data in a standard format

Contact: 8bfr.network@gmail.com

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Users under 13 are not permitted to use the main platform. The KidZone section requires parental consent and is governed by stricter privacy controls. Parents can manage and delete their child's data at any time through the Parent Dashboard.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage for session tokens, UI preferences (avatar toggle, theme), and AI usage tracking. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.
