📖 About 8BFR

🎵 8BFR Music Network

A community built by creators, for creators. Where music meets culture meets opportunity.

🎯 Our Mission

8BFR exists to give independent artists, beatmakers, producers, and music lovers a platform that actually works for them. No algorithms burying your content. No gatekeepers. Just music, community, and real connections.

We believe every creator deserves a space to share their art, find collaborators, and build a following — whether you're just starting out or you've been grinding for years.

💡 What Makes 8BFR Different

100% Royalties: Artists keep every dollar from their sales. Our 80/20 honor system means creators are trusted, not taxed.

Real Community: Feed, chat rooms, DMs, stories, playlists, dedications — this isn't just a music player, it's a social network built around music.

Beat Store: Producers sell beats with their own license agreements. Buyers pay directly via PayPal.

Creator Tools: AI-powered tools for album covers, music promotion, translation, and more.

Radio: Continuous play of community music. Every uploaded song gets heard.

Games: Pool, arcade, sports, music games — because community is about more than just music.

👤 The Founder

8BFR was created by James Siburt — a music lover who got tired of platforms that don't care about independent artists. Built from scratch on a phone using Termux, Supabase, and pure determination.

This platform is a labor of love. Every feature, every page, every line of code was designed with creators in mind.

🛡️ Age-Based Access

8BFR takes safety seriously. We use a three-tier age system to protect younger users while giving everyone the best experience:

🧸 KidZone (12 & Under) — A fully separate, moderated creative space. Parent-verified accounts only. No main site access, no DMs, no purchases.

🔵 Teen (13-15) — Main site access with parental consent. Restricted features: no tipping, no purchases, no live. DMs limited to teens + parent. 2-hour daily default.

🟣 Full Access (16+) — Everything unlocked.

This policy complies with COPPA and emerging state laws requiring parental consent for users under 16. Read our full age policy →

👁️ Safety & Monitoring

The platform owner monitors all public and private communications for safety purposes, including DMs, community chat, and all KidZone activity. This may be done anonymously. Any illegal activity — especially involving minors — results in an immediate permanent ban and law enforcement referral.

We take child safety extremely seriously. See our Community Rules and Terms of Service for full details.
