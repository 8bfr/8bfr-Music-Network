💡 Writing Tips

📐 Structure First

Before writing, create an outline. Use the Outline Builder above to organize your thoughts into sections. A clear structure makes writing 3x faster.

✂️ Edit Ruthlessly

First drafts are meant to be messy. Write freely, then use the Proofreader and Rewriter to polish. Cut any sentence that doesn't serve the reader.

🎯 Hook Your Reader

Your opening sentence decides if someone keeps reading. Use the Headline Generator to test different angles. Start with a question, bold claim, or vivid scene.

🗣️ Read Aloud

If a sentence sounds awkward spoken, it reads awkward too. Read your work aloud to catch rhythm issues, repetition, and unclear phrasing.

📊 Know Your Audience

Music journalism reads differently than poetry. Match your tone to your audience. Use the Rewriter tool to adjust style — professional, casual, poetic, or bold.
