8BFR's story began in his early years, when he discovered a love for old school Snoop, Dr. Dre, and Ice Cube. He wrote raps and poems, immersing himself in the culture and rhythm of hip-hop.

It wasn't until he was shot and suffered from PTSD that he began beatmaking and rapping seriously. Music became not just a passion, but a form of healing and expression. With the support of his friends and family, 8BFR began to take his creativity to the next level.

8BFR comes from a musically and artistically talented family, though none of them are famous. His main inspiration is his mom, and his drive to make extra money from downloads is what motivates him.
