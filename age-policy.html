Age Policy

18+ ADULTS ONLY

8BFR is an Adults-Only Network

8BFR Music Network is a social platform for adults 18 years of age or older. You must be at least 18 to create an account, post content, chat, or use any feature of the site.

To be clear: "adults only" refers to membership age, not content. 8BFR is not an adult-content or pornographic platform. Explicit sexual content is not permitted (see our Rules). We simply require that all members are legal adults.

By signing up, you confirm under penalty of account termination that you are 18 years of age or older. Accounts found to belong to minors will be removed.

Why 18+?

8BFR includes open community chat, direct messaging, a member feed, donation links, and creator monetization features. These are designed for adults and are not appropriate for minors. Restricting membership to adults keeps the community consistent, lawful, and safer for everyone.

Age Verification

At signup, every member must confirm they are 18 or older. 8BFR may additionally require government-issued photo ID verification to confirm age where appropriate. Providing false age information is grounds for immediate account removal.

Reporting

If you believe an account belongs to someone under 18, please report it immediately through the in-app report tools or by contacting us. We act quickly on credible reports.
