Reply to comments on your posts and tracks. This increases engagement, which increases your points, which increases your visibility. It's a positive cycle!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I buy points?

No. Points can only be earned through genuine activity and engagement. This keeps the platform fair and rewards real creators, not those with the biggest budgets.

Do points expire?

No. Once you earn points, they're yours forever. However, long periods of inactivity (6+ months) may reduce your visibility temporarily until you post again.

Can I lose points?

Yes, but only for violations like spam, fake engagement, or plagiarism. Follow the rules and be genuine, and you'll never lose points.

How often is the algorithm updated?

The algorithm updates in real-time as you earn points and get engagement. There's no delay ‚Äî your activity immediately affects your visibility.

Does deleting content remove points?

No. Points earned from deleted content stay with you. However, the content obviously won't continue earning new points or engagement.
