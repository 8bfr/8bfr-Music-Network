How Ads Work on 8BFR

Simple, creator-friendly ad rotation. We always keep at least 5 visuals running so the site stays alive even when no paid spots are active.

  1. There are always 5 ad slots rotating in the home hero carousel. If there are no paid ads, we use house / placeholder art.
  2. When someone buys an ad, their artwork replaces a placeholder in one or more slots for a set number of days.
  3. When a campaign ends, that slot automatically falls back to our default banner until it‚Äôs sold again.
  4. Users can pause the carousel or use arrows / swipe to move between ads. When they tap an ad, they land on this page with the full-size art.
  5. Most campaigns also get shout-outs in the community feed, radio, or featured sections (depending on the package).
