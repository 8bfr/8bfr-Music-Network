💰 How the Beat Store Works

Beatmakers upload their beats and set their own prices. Each beatmaker uploads their own license agreements with their beats. There are no preset tiers — the beatmaker controls everything.

When you click + Buy, you'll see the payment options the beatmaker has set. The transaction is directly between you and the beatmaker via PayPal. 8BFR is a gateway — not a party to the transaction.

⚠️ 80/20 Honor System: Beatmakers receive 100% of the payment at point of sale, then send 20% to [email protected]. If a beatmaker consistently fails to honor the 20%, the owner reserves the right to disable their selling privileges.
⚠️ Disclaimer: 8BFR, 8BFR Social Network, and James J. Siburt are NOT responsible for refunds, license disputes, or copyright issues. Artists and beatmakers are solely responsible for their own license agreements, content rights, and customer disputes. 8BFR is a platform, not a party to any transaction.
