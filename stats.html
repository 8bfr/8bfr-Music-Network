📈 How Points Work

Each action on 8BFR earns algorithm points that boost your visibility: posting (+5), getting a like (+2), getting a comment (+3), getting a follow (+4), uploading a song (+10), getting a reshare (+5). Higher tiers mean your content appears more often in feeds and recommendations. Tiers: 🥉 Bronze (0-99), 🥈 Silver (100-499), 🥇 Gold (500-1999), 💎 Platinum (2000-4999), 👑 Diamond (5000+).
