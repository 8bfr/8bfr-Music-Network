🧮 How Charts Work

Charts are powered by the 8BFR algorithm which weighs multiple factors:

🔥 Hot 50 — Combined score: plays (40%) + likes (25%) + shares (20%) + recency (15%)

📈 Trending — Biggest score increase in the time period

🆕 New Releases — Songs uploaded in the last 7 days, ranked by early engagement

🎵 Top Beats — Instrumentals only, ranked by producer plays + purchases

❤️ Most Liked — Pure like count ranking

▶️ Most Played — Pure play count ranking
